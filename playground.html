Crossover of “Heroes of the Storms” and “Magic: the Gathering”

We share this passion for Blizzards' "Heroes of the Storm" and Wizard's "Magic: The Gathering" games and our motivation was to make printable high-quality cards to be used in a casual multiplayer commander table.

This is a work in progress still. We are reviewing each card, tweaking some unbalanced ones and refactoring others.

Enjoy!
